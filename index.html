  • 03
    Viewport Testing: Tap [Desktop], [Tablet], [Mobile] buttons in the preview header to verify fluid responsiveness.

📋 Save Links & Request Customization

  • 01
    Copy Template Link: Click Copy next to the Launch button to copy the direct URL of your favorite design to clipboard.
  • 02
    Discuss on Forum: Scroll down to Client Feedback Board, select the target template, and post your questions or custom layout ideas.
  • 03
    Direct Zalo Chat: Or for the fastest support, click the floating Personalization badge or message Duy directly via Zalo at 0979605867.

  • 01

  • 02

  • 03
    Custom Domains: Duy provides free configuration for custom domains (e.g. `yourname.com`) to make your digital identity fully professional.
